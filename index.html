  • My research interests

    • Computational chemistry & AI

      I am constantly use DFT and MD to find suitable catalysts and solvents for process energy applications. I am also developing generative AI to predict new crystal structures for plethora of applications (with a quirky approach :P). Happy material hunting!

    • Control & design strategies

      I strongly believe that a mature product or piece of equipment should integrate automation and leverage multiple technologies. I actively work on hunting down boring process to automate them to an extent that it requires no human intervention. To complement this, I design innovative equipment that overcomes current limitations, utilizing CFD analysis and rapid prototyping norms.

    • Reaction Engg &
      Electrochemistry

      I Passionately use electrochemistry as my primary tool for almost everything!! The principles of reaction engineering provide a rational framework to address complex problems, offering innovative and efficient solutions. Reaction engineering approach is a blessing!

  • Projects

    A. At Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT), Mumbai; 2023 to present

    1. Bifunctional catalysts for OER and HER: investigating MoS₂-MoO₃ composites on MXene and Ni-Co LDH supports.

    2. Development of thin film evaporator inspired top flow electrolyzer.

    3. Photocatalytic CO2 reduction using 2-D material catalysts

    4. Generative AI to predict energy materials.

    5. Synthesis and molecular dynamics simulation of 6FDA-TMPDA membrane for separation of NO from a mixture of N2 and O2

    B. At M.S Ramaiah Institute of Technology (MSRIT), Bengaluru; 2021 to 2023

    1. Synthesized and evaluated the performance of Ba2+ substituted lanthanum cobalt nickelate nanocrystals as an anode material for oxygen evolution reaction in alkaline water electrolysis.

    2. Minor project on a detailed NPK fertilizer production through nitro phosphate route, evaluated its thermodynamic feasibility, performed material and energy balance, designed required industrial equipment, created a plant layout, and completed a cost estimation of the plant.

    3. Carried out the synthesis of Barium Lanthanate nanocrystals using combustion synthesis. Carried out the various types of material characterization of the synthesized materials.
